Bishop Family Vacation 2017

The places we visited on our vacation after the 19 hour road trip.

Rehoboth Beach, DE

The first destination my family and I visited was Rehoboth Beach, Delaware. Rehoboth Beach is a great family beach and the first beach my youngest daughter had ever been to. We snacked on the famous boardwalk fries from Thrasher's. After building sand castles, dipping in the ocean, and getting some sun we grabbed icecream cones and walked on the boardwalk. We had a day in the sun with ocean breezes, what a great time!

Assateague Island, MD

My family and I went to see the wild horses of Assateague Island National Seashore in Maryland. The horses swim from the mainland to the island in early spring and swim back to the mainland in the fall. You can relax on the white sandy beaches and have wild horses walking past you. A must see if you are on the Maryland coast.

Ocean City, MD

My favorite beach on the east coast, the beach I visited as a child every summer. I was able to bring my family to enjoy Ocean City, Maryland and we made sand castles and played in the ocean. After we had too much sun, we went to the boardwalk and bought two hermit crabs to bring home. We also purchased salt water taffy from Dolle's
